Study Rules That Actually Work

⏱Start your 3h block at the same time every day. Consistency beats intensity.
🔥First 30 min = review. Never start fresh. Always revisit yesterday first.
⌨️Type every code example yourself. Copy-paste kills retention in embedded.
📸Photo every breadboard circuit. You will forget the wiring at 2am.
🐛Spend at least 20 min debugging before googling. Debugging IS the skill.
📝Last 15 min = write 3 sentences: what you did, what broke, what you learned.
